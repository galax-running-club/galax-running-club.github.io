Independence Freedom Day 5K

Registration Form: PDF | Microsoft Word

Schedule

  • 7:00 - 7:45: Race day registration (T-shirt not guaranteed)
  • 7:45: Kids fun run (1/4 mile in parking lot)
  • 8:00: 5K
  • 10:00: Fourth of July Parade
  • 10:30 (approximately): Awards at old courthouse mc stand ( can pick up medals before the parade if results are available)

Entries

  • $15-If postmarked by June 22nd (T-shirt included)
